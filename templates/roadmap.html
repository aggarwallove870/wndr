About

The Company

WNDR powers browser-based premium multiplayer meta-games, virtual events and immersive open worlds distributed on any connected device with a simple web link. Our Streaming engine distributes with the “click-to-share” ease of a Youtube video. WNDR is the next evolution of scalable, blockchain-powered, social-focused gaming in the metaverse. The company’s first official game title, “The Colosseum,” is set in the futuristic world of WNDR.LAND, WNDRs streaming technology enables small teams to create high-end game experiences without a traditional publisher or game studio. WNDR represents the next evolution of scalable, blockchain-powered, social-focused gaming in the metaverse.

What does WNDR offer?

  • The ability to deliver 3D immersive experiences purely via the web without a download or special hardware.
  • The ability to distribute these experiences via social media platforms, text, or email.
  • Cloud streaming technology enables small teams to create and monetize high-end AAA-quality game experience in UnrealEngine without the need for a traditional publisher and game studio partner.
  • The ability to interact with a live crowd during the experience (via text or voice chat).
  • The ability to sell physical or virtual goods, including NFTs, directly from the immersive setting.
  • Unlimited potential to create vast hyper-realistic open-worlds, games and experiences.
